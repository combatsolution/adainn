Intelligent Document Processing (IDP)

Technologies: Document AI, Tesseract OCR, OpenAI API, Apache NiFi, PostgreSQL

Problem : Manual document handling is time-consuming, error-prone, and delays operational workflows in finance and enterprise processes.

Nabni AI

Industry: Real Estate

Technologies: Document AI, Open AI, LangGraph, Qdrant DB, PostgreSQL

Problem : Real estate professionals face manual, time-consuming processes when dealing with regulatory and legal paperwork. Errors, delays, and inefficiencies in reviewing and extracting info from contracts slow down transactions and impact accuracy.

Solution: Nabni’s platform automates document reading, extracting critical data and flags key clauses instantly. It accelerates deal workflows, reduces manual effort, ensures compliance, and empowers users to make faster, smarter decisions.

Mammoth Technologies

Industry: Mining

Technologies: Python, Docker, REST APIs

Problem : Mining companies often face high downtime and asset losses due to poor tool visibility and unplanned maintenance.

Solution: Cloud-based AI-enabled tool management platform that integrates IoT sensors for real-time asset tracking, predictive maintenance scheduling using ML algorithms, and analytics dashboards for usage optimization. The system reduces unplanned downtime and extends equipment lifecycle.
